Tailored Career Management & Guidance Package for High School Learners

This comprehensive package is designed to help high school learners navigate their career choices, university applications, and bursary opportunities. It provides step-by-step guidance, tools, and resources to empower learners to make informed decisions about their future.

1. Career Exploration & Guidance

  • Help learners identify their interests, strengths, and potential career paths.
  • Guide learners on how to research careers (e.g., using platforms like LinkedIn, Glassdoor, or government labour statistics).
  • Provide a list of in-demand careers and emerging industries (e.g., AI, renewable energy, healthcare, etc.).

2. University Applications

  • Assist learners in selecting the right universities and courses, and guide them through the application process.
  • Share resources like university rankings, open-day schedules, and virtual campus tours.
  • Help learners match their career goals with relevant university qualifications.
  • Discuss the differences between degrees, diplomas, and vocational training.
  • Provide a timeline for university applications (e.g., deadlines for universities).
  • Assist with gathering required documents (e.g., transcripts, recommendation letters, and identification).
  • Confirmation of registered and accredited private institutions.

3. Bursary & Financial Aid Applications

  • Help learners apply for funding for their tertiary education.
  • Provide a list of available bursaries, scholarships, and financial aid options (e.g., government, private sector, and university-specific bursaries).
  • Share websites and databases (e.g., Fastweb, Scholarships.com, or local bursary portals).
  • Provide a checklist of required documents (e.g., proof of income, academic records, and motivational letters).

4. Preparation for the Future

  • Teach learners how to create a professional CV and LinkedIn profile.
  • Conduct mock interviews to build confidence and improve interview skills.

5. Ongoing Support & Follow-Up

  • Provide continuous support to ensure learners stay on track.

This package can be delivered through a combination of in-person sessions, online resources, and one-on-one mentoring. By providing structured guidance and ongoing support, high school learners will be well-equipped to make informed decisions about their future and achieve their academic and career goals.
